Pool cleaning questions, answered

Do I need to be home during service?
No. We need access to the backyard and pool equipment. Please secure pets before we arrive.
Do you bring chemicals?
We can use your chemicals or include them in your quote - tell us your preference when you request a price.
Is this weekly service only?
Yes. Weekly service keeps Florida pools stable and helps prevent algae. One-time deep cleans are available before weekly service starts.
Do you service above-ground pools and hot tubs?
Yes. We service in-ground pools, above-ground pools, and hot tubs. Mention your setup in the quote form.
What should I do before you arrive?
Keep the water at the proper level — we take care of the rest.
Do you do pool repairs or remodeling?
No, but we work with partners who can quote and help with your pool.
Can you remove rust or calcium stains from the pool surface?
Yes. It requires a specific estimate depending on the damage.
